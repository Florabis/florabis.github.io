### **1\. Fighting Outside of the Military**

* Home defense situations  
  - Owners may defend their property, if found actively being broken into.  
  - Home owners must provide screenshot evidence of the break in.  
* Self defense situations  
  - Person may protect themselves if being actively attacked with a weapon  
  - Weapons are tools that inflict the combat tags and have an attack modifier  
  - Fighting back is a last resort option, especially if in a foreign state or nation.

### **2\. Apply to the Florabis Military**

* Florabis citizens may apply to the Florabis military  
* Details about Florabis military can be found within the specific discord channels

### **3\. Apply to be a Florabis Judge**

* Florabis citizens may apply to be a Florabis judge  
* Create a ticket in Florabis discord server

### **1\. Creating an Event**

* Plan out the following:  
  - Event name  
  - Date and time  
  - Location  
  - Activities and details  
  - Flyer and marketing graphics (optional)  
* Post the planned information to the \#citizen-event channel

### **2\. Advertising an Event**

* Create a ticket in the Florabis discord  
* Request an announcement and event to be made for the event  
* Soberante, Mayor Alcadesa, and / or Councilor of Culture and Heritage can approve the event advertisement  
  - Soberante and Mayor Alcadesa may skip getting approval

### **1\. Creating State Projects**

* Anyone may propose a state project which is any build or task that may take longer than 2 weeks and multiple citizens to complete  
* Create a post in \#state-projects that includes:  
  - The issue and proposed solution to issue  
  - Details and plans  
  - Coordinates of build site  
  - Screenshots of build in creative, build inspiration, or schematic  
* Approval from Soberante and / or Alcadesa Mayor
