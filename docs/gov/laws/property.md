### **1\. Ways to Acquire Property**

* Given property by an individual or the government as a gift or reward for achieving the next tier.
* Sold property by an individual or the government.

### **2\. Acquiring Building Permission**

* Create a post in \#building-permissions that includes:
    - Coordinates of build site
    - Screenshots of build in creative, build inspiration, or schematic
* Approval from Soberante, Alcadesa Mayor, and / or Councilor of Works & Infrastructure

### **3\. Acquiring Building Permission for Confidential Projects**

* Create a post in \#sensitive-discussion that includes:
    - Coordinates of build site
    - Screenshots of build in creative, build inspiration, or schematic
* Approval from Soberante and / or Alcadesa Mayor

### **4\. Reinforcement**

* All buildings reinforced on the Florabuilds layer
* Beds, chests, and doors are reinforced on a personal layer
